A POEM ABOUT A BOY CALLED TIM

Formally illustrated from Adam to Tim during childhood

I had a little brother,
his name was Tiny Tim,
I put him in the bathtub
to teach him how to swim;

He drank up all the water,
he ate up all the soap,
He died last night
with a bubble in his throat.

In came the doctor,
in came the nurse,
In came the lady
with the alligator purse;

Dead said the doctor,
dead said the nurse,
Dead said the lady
with the alligator purse;

Out went the doctor,
out went the nurse,
Out went the lady
with the alligator purse;
